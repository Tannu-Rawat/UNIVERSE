Commonly Faced Doubts

Can I chat privately with a specific student or professor?

Yes, the chat application allows you to chat one-on-one with any student or professor registered on the platform. Just search for their name and start a conversation instantly.

What features are available in the group chat?

Group chats can be used for class discussions, project collaborations, or club activities. You can share messages, files, and resources within the group. The chat history is accessible to all group members, even if they join later, so everyone stays informed.

How can I get help or information about the college through the app?

You can use our built-in chatbot to get quick answers regarding college information, such as academic schedules, campus events, or details about specific resources. Just type your question, and the chatbot will assist you!
